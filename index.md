# AI for Business Innovation: A Comprehensive Textbook for ISM 6427C
:::{figure} ./images/cover.png
:width: 300px
:align: center
:::


Welcome to **AI for Business Innovation: A Comprehensive Textbook for ISM 6427C** by Dr. Ernesto Lee, DBA.

This textbook serves as the primary course text for ISM 6427C at Florida Atlantic University. The book takes a practitioner-first approach: every chapter pairs conceptual frameworks with hands-on tool mastery, progressing from foundational prompt engineering through full-stack agentic application development.

Students will use free and affordable tools—Google AI Studio, Claude, Claude Code, Antigravity, Vapi.ai, and open-source frameworks—to build real business solutions across eight progressive modules.
